OUR STORY

Online shopping sites contain infinite brands and products. Navigating through those sites for hours to find a gift is hard & time consuming. So we gathered our team to put an end to this!

We created a simple, unique and easy gift recommendation engine for you, which recommends products of popular brands.

Donavi only asks you a couple of questions to tailor the perfect gift for loved one – for you to enjoy the satisfaction of buying a good gift, the easy way.

OUR VISION

Everyone loves a well-thought gift. But getting creative when choosing a gift is hard... And there are many options to choose from... We don’t think it has to be that way.

We understand that you might be busy or undecisive when it comes to gifting. So, we decided to say no to a disorganized and overwhelming gifting site.

With Donavi, never spend frustrated & confused hours again on choosing gifts! You’ll be out of here happy & satisfied in under 10 minutes.
